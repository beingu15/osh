# **App Name**: The OSH Atelier

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Elegant display of The OSH Atelier's brand, services (Professional Makeup, Custom Outfit Design, Saree Draping, Saree Box Folding), and artistic aesthetic through high-quality imagery and video.
- Portfolio Gallery: Display high-resolution images of makeup and costume designs. Allow users to filter by category.
- Service Details Pages: Dedicated pages for each service, showcasing descriptions, pricing options, and booking details. Integrated with the scheduling system.
- SEO Optimization: Implement SEO strategies including schema markup for products and services, optimized meta descriptions, alt tags for images, and fast page load times.
- Contact and Booking Form: A contact form for inquiries and a booking system for makeup and costume design services. Display working hours, location of studio, and allow for easy communication with the Atelier. Provide immediate estimate for work requested using generative AI to turn the work requested in an itemized statement of work and pricing.
- Blog/Inspiration Section: Share behind-the-scenes content, makeup tips, outfit ideas, and client stories to engage and attract customers.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Gold (#D4AF37), inspired by the black and gold logo to evoke luxury and sophistication.
- Background color: Off-White (#F5F5DC) provides a clean and elegant backdrop to make the gold and black elements stand out, in keeping with a light color scheme.
- Accent color: Dark Brown (#8B4513), used sparingly to add depth and richness to certain elements and calls to action, complementing the analogous primary color.
- Font: 'Belleza' (sans-serif) for headlines, offering a stylish and design-aligned look, paired with 'Alegreya' (serif) for body text to ensure readability and a touch of elegance.
- Use minimalist, custom-designed icons in gold to represent different services and features.
- Responsive layout with a focus on visual storytelling. Large, high-quality images and videos to showcase the artistry.
- Subtle animations on page transitions and image hover effects to enhance user engagement.
